Zombie Fire Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Zombie Fire does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Local Save Data

The game stores progress locally on the player's device, including level stars, gold, experience, selected equipment, and weapon upgrades. This data is not transmitted to a server.

No Tracking

The current build does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, account login, in-app purchases, push notifications, third-party tracking, or cloud services.

Future Changes

If a future version adds online services, analytics, ads, purchases, leaderboards, cloud saves, or accounts, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure must be updated before release.

Support

Use the support link listed on the App Store product page.
